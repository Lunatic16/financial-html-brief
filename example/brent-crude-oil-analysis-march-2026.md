## Business Overview

Brent Crude is the world's most widely used oil price benchmark, pricing approximately two-thirds of all internationally traded crude oil. It trades on the Intercontinental Exchange (ICE) in London as futures contracts and serves as the primary global reference for waterborne crude oil.
